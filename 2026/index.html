About the Conference

A global stage for the future of computing

RICE-2026 brings together researchers, academicians and industry practitioners to explore innovative ideas and emerging technological developments — through keynote talks, hands-on workshops and peer-reviewed technical sessions.

Organized by CMR Engineering College, Hyderabad and technically sponsored by IEEE, RICE carries a decade-long legacy of quality and international collaboration.
