About the Conference

A decade-long legacy of bringing the global intelligent-computing community together.

Overview

Where ideas meet the ocean

The 11th International Conference on Research in Intelligent Computing in Engineering (RICE-2026) aims to gauge the emerging trends in computational methods, data science, machine learning and their real-time applications.

It facilitates rich interaction among researchers to explore innovative ideas and emerging technological developments — across two immersive days of keynote talks, hands-on workshops and peer-reviewed technical sessions on the shores of Goa.

Organized by CMR Engineering College, Hyderabad and technically sponsored by IEEE, RICE has grown from its inaugural 2016 edition into a truly international gathering.
